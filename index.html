Treatments that I offer
  • Gel Nails * €25

  • Gel nails are made from polymer resin. The application is done in three phases. The gel is cured with the help of UV light. First a base layer is applied, which will cure under a UV light, then the second layer, which is hardened in the same way. The nail artist then applies a topcoat, which must also be cured under the UV lamp

  • Gel Nails Fill-in €17.50

  • Like acrylics, gels grow out with your nails and need to be filled in every two to three weeks. We will gently file down the gel edge closest to your nail bed, and then fill in the empty area between your nail bed and the existing gel nail.

    Removal: You can remove most gel nails by soaking them in nail polish remover. Some nail-sized wraps are filled with nail polish remover, which can loosen the artificial nails enough for removal, without drying out your hands.

  • Acrylic-Gel Nails * €35.00

  • A gel and acrylic in one. Stronger than gel and more flexible than acrylic. Easier to apply than other nail systems.

  • Acrylic-Gel Fill-in €20.00

  • We will gently file down the acrylic edge closest to your nail bed, then fill in the empty area between your nail bed and the existing acrylic nail.

  • Gel Colour €17.50

  • We will apply any gel colour and shape

  • We will colour your nail with any colour and shape. Prior to that we will apply a shot manicure, remove cuticles and and moisture-regulating lotions.
