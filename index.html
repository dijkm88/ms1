about

My name is Nasima Dhoeman, and two years ago, I started with my certification for becoming a professional manicurist.

Today I can say I am a fully qualified, trained and certified Nail Artist. Besides this, I am also offering make-up and hair styling for every occasion. Please have a look at the various certifications that I've received, and if you'd like to know more, please look at the extensive list of treatments that I offer, and in case you have any questions, please do contact me for more information.

I am now taking bookings for appointments, and besides working from my address, I can also meet on location.
